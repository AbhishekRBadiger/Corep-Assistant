RULE 1: COMMON EQUITY TIER 1 (CET1) CAPITAL
CET1 capital includes:
- Ordinary shares issued by the bank
- Share premium (extra money paid above share face value)
- Retained earnings (profits kept in the business)
- Other reserves

RULE 2: DEDUCTIONS FROM CET1
You must subtract these from CET1:
- Intangible assets (like goodwill, software)
- Deferred tax assets
- Losses from current year

RULE 3: TIER 1 CAPITAL CALCULATION
Tier 1 Capital = CET1 Capital + Additional Tier 1 Capital

RULE 4: CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS
Banks must maintain:
- CET1 ratio of at least 4.5%
- Tier 1 ratio of at least 6%
- Total capital ratio of at least 8%
